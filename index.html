Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend visiting every 6 months for routine cleanings and checkups. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their oral health condition. Regular visits help prevent serious dental problems and maintain optimal oral health.
Children should have their first dental visit by their first birthday or within 6 months of their first tooth appearing. Early visits help establish good oral hygiene habits and allow us to monitor proper tooth development.
Our digital X-rays use very low radiation levels and are generally safe during pregnancy when proper shielding is used. However, we typically postpone non-emergency X-rays until after delivery unless absolutely necessary for treatment.
Most root canal treatments can be completed in 1-3 visits, depending on the complexity of the case. Each appointment typically lasts 60-90 minutes. Our advanced techniques ensure efficient treatment with minimal discomfort.
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI payments, and bank transfers. We also offer flexible payment plans for extensive treatments and work with most dental insurance providers.
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency dental services for severe pain, trauma, or urgent dental issues. Call our emergency line and we'll arrange immediate care to address your dental emergency.
With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last 20-25 years or even a lifetime. Success depends on good oral hygiene, regular dental visits, and avoiding habits like smoking that can affect healing.
Your first visit includes a comprehensive oral examination, digital X-rays if needed, discussion of your dental history and concerns, and a personalized treatment plan. We'll also provide oral hygiene instructions and answer all your questions.
Professional teeth whitening is safe for most adults. However, it's not recommended for pregnant/nursing women, children under 16, or people with certain dental conditions. We'll evaluate your suitability during consultation.
We specialize in anxiety-free dentistry using gentle techniques, sedation options, and a calming environment. Communicate your concerns with us, and we'll customize your treatment to ensure maximum comfort throughout your visit.
Professional cleaning includes plaque and tartar removal, tooth polishing, fluoride treatment, oral examination, and personalized oral hygiene instructions. We use advanced tools to ensure thorough cleaning while maintaining your comfort.
Timing varies based on individual healing and bone condition. Immediate implants can sometimes be placed right after extraction, while others may require 3-6 months of healing. We'll determine the best timeline during your consultation.

Privacy Policy

At Sakthi Dental Clinic, we are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal and medical information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information including name, contact details, medical history, dental records, insurance information, and treatment records necessary for providing quality dental care.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for providing dental treatment, appointment scheduling, insurance processing, communication regarding your care, and maintaining accurate medical records as required by law.

Information Protection

We implement strict security measures including encrypted storage, limited access protocols, secure transmission methods, and regular security audits to protect your sensitive information.

Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information except as required for your treatment, with your explicit consent, for insurance processing, or as mandated by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to access your records, request corrections, withdraw consent for non-essential communications, and receive information about how your data is used.
